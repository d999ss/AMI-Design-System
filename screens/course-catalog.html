Course Catalog

Courses

Functional Area: Courses · Variants: 3 sections · Breakpoints: 4

Browseable catalog of available training courses. Features tabbed navigation (In Progress, Required, Recommended, All), filterable card grid, and search functionality. Course cards show progress, brand association, and completion status.
