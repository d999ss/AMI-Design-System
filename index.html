Critical Gaps 12 Items

Issues that need resolution before engineering handoff. See full remediation roadmap →

6+ Color Naming Systems

Semantic, Text & Icons, AMIO, /Primary, color/surface, Tertiary. Need consolidation to one standard.

Incomplete Elevation System

Only Elevation 2 defined. Missing Elevation 1 (cards), 3 (modals), 4 (tooltips).

3 Spacing Naming Systems

Space/8 vs Spacing/8 vs spacing/08. Three conventions across components.

No Breakpoint or Grid Tokens

Viewport variants exist but no shared breakpoint or grid column tokens defined.

Legacy Font (Roboto) Still Active

Footer and old Tabs still use Roboto. Migration to Graphik needed.

No Motion / Animation Tokens

No duration, easing, or transition tokens. Needed for consistent interactions.
